OralScan

AI-Powered Accessible Dental Care for Older Adults

Problem: Lack of Accessible Preventive Dental Care

Who: Seniors who cannot access regular oral healthcare.

Where: Widespread issue but particularly prevalent in rural or low-income areas.

Why: Financial, geographic, and systemic barriers, as well as limitations in Medicare coverage.

Statistics and Data

  • 96% of seniors experience dental caries (cavities).
  • 20% of seniors suffer from untreated dental decay.
  • 68% of older adults have periodontal (gum) disease, a major cause of tooth loss.

Early Conceptual Design

We have developed a conceptual prototype of a mobile app for photo capture and integrated AI models with dental professionals. As shown in the figure below, the app consists of two main components: a front-end application that interacts with the user and a back-end server responsible for authentication, secure data storage, and analysis of the data.

  • The front-end app is developed using React Native, supporting both Android and iOS platforms.
  • Users enter clinical information and capture images via guided prompts.
  • AI-based models detect regions of interest (e.g., caries, ulcers) and segment the objects in less than 5 seconds.

The back-end server handles AI model processing, securely stores data, and ensures privacy.

Aim and Development Plan

AI Algorithm Development

We are developing a neural network algorithm that incorporates oral images to predict the risk of oral diseases by leveraging models like YOLO and SAM. Image features will be extracted using a ResNet50-based CNN.

Secure Server and Fairness Assessment

The server will handle tasks like segmentation, prediction model training, and secure data storage. We will also focus on fairness assessment through demographic representation analysis and bias mitigation techniques.

Web-Based Dashboard

A dashboard will allow administrative users to monitor system performance, bug reports, and user feedback in real-time, pulling data from the server's relational database.

FAQs

OralScan combines AI-driven diagnostics with a user-friendly mobile platform tailored for seniors and underserved populations. It uses smartphone cameras to capture intraoral images, enabling detection of oral health issues like cavities, gingivitis, and ulcers. It bridges healthcare gaps by providing real-time diagnostic feedback and self-care recommendations or professional care alerts.

OralScan integrates advanced AI diagnostics with mHealth accessibility, focusing on equity. Challenges like consistent image quality and biases in AI algorithms were addressed with advanced image quality assessments and fairness-aware AI, ensuring reliable diagnostics across diverse demographics.

Existing solutions like telemedicine apps and AI tools (e.g., AICaries9) focus on specific aspects but lack comprehensive oral health screening. OralScan fills this gap with robust datasets and real-time, at-home assessments.

Technological Impact: Real-time diagnostics, unique datasets, and EHR integration enable remote patient monitoring and optimize resources.

Societal Impact: Empowers underserved populations with accessible tools, encourages proactive oral health behaviors, and promotes healthcare equity.
